About Me

Hello! My name is Louella Landicho. I am a Lead Creative Designer for a biopharmaceutical company based in California. I love design and technology. One of my dream is to become a well rounded UI/UX, web designer/developer/engineer, and now, I am working toward it.

I was born in Philippines. I came to the U.S. year 1992 and have lived here ever since. I like volleyball, music, bike, long drive/walks, outdoors and travel. Particularly like to take vacations to exotic and historic countries yearly. I have visited parts of Central America, Europe, and Asia. On those travels, I like to try their local foods, coffee, beers and get lost. I am amazed and blessed to experienced different culture and places.
